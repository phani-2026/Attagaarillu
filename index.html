Our Story

Where Every Meal Feels Like Home

Inspired by the warmth of meals prepared in a traditional Telugu household, Attagaarillu was born from a heartfelt desire — to bring the nostalgic taste of amma's and attagaaru's cooking to everyone's table. Every recipe is a tribute to the culinary heritage of Andhra Pradesh, crafted with the same love and authentic spices that have been passed down through generations.

🎯

Our Mission

To serve authentic Telugu home-style meals that preserve traditional flavors while offering a comforting dining experience to every customer.

🌟

Our Vision

To become a trusted brand for authentic Andhra cuisine across multiple cities while maintaining the warmth and authenticity of home cooking.
